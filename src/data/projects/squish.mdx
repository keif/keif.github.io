Squish is a privacy-friendly image optimization service that makes it easy to resize, compress, and convert images without uploading them to third-party servers.

## Features

- **Client-side processing** - Your images never leave your device
- **Multiple formats** - Support for JPEG, PNG, WebP, and AVIF
- **Smart compression** - Optimize file size while maintaining quality
- **Batch processing** - Handle multiple images at once
- **API available** - Integrate into your own workflows
